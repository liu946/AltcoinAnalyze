Is there any relationship between digital currency value and code development?
Let's dive in

#1  DIGITAL GRAPHICS

Showing Data

First, the process download repositories of digital coins. The data hava two sources , the repositories (with GitStats changed) and GitHub API . Considering the different meaning of items, we present a normaliaztion and try to show it visually.

Every column is an item that may have relationship with the activity of project development. We divide every data num by the maximum of it's item to normalize it. According to this, we can give them an degree to how this item(column) of this coin(row) is, comparing with others. The lager data has a deeper background.

Heat weight chart

We thought about doing a summary of the all items of one digital coin and give them a weight to present it's contribution to the value of the code. Besides sum the normalized data item, we also calculated the area of the radar graphic (as RadarArea in the GRAPHICS and angle as the weight), and product of all items' power(as Mul in the GRAPHICS and power as the weight).

#5  BEST WEIGHT

MAX-Correlation Coefficent

We use the correlation coefficent to find the relation between value we pridict and the real market profile and use this method to find the best weights to predict the market value.

We calculate the correlation coefficent (marked as r in Math) with every possible weight option from 0% to 100% and get it's r. Because weights' sum equal to 1,on the left hand we use 2 dimensions for them .(X for star, Y for commit and issue is 100%-X-Y) We use Z axis to show R. The weights are %26(star) %0(commit) %74(issue). We see the commit is on more help to increase r.

So we painted a new graphic and remove commit on the right hand.

#6  PREDICT

Relation

We use the best weight to predict altcoins marketcap.

We use graph to show the relationship between develop activity(marketcap prediction) and real marketcap.

Blue dots represent altcoins' marketcap prediction, greed dots represent real marketcap, X-coordinate represent different coins.

From the pridiction-marketcap graph, we found prediction and marketcap has no strong relationship.

When develop activity increase, the marketcap does not change a lot, and somtimes it even decrease.

Bitcoin is an exception. But does it have representativeness?

In conclusion, there might be many other factors that will influence an altcoin's marketcap more than develop activity.

#7  FURTHER RESEARCH

Rank

We think the relationship between develop activity and rank of altcoin is more meaningful.

We found the best weight: 2%(ActiveDays) 0%(Forks) 14%(Subscribers) 12%(Open issues) 72%(ActiveDays Percentage).

Under this weight, develop activity has MAX correlation coefficent(-0.4806) with rank.

Not a strong relationship, right? And from the graph below, we found the mean error of predict rank is 6.2.

So, we have more evidence to prove the develop activity of an altcoin does not have much influence to its marketcap/rank.
